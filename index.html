Integrating Lifestyle Changes with Homeopathic Treatment for Optimal PCOS Management

PCOS occurs, or a patient is said to be suffering from PCOS, when there is excess androgens in the body. Androgen are male hormones, and the presence of excess androgen leads to the formation of small cysts on the ovaries. Due to a hormonal disorder, it is the ovaries that produce the androgens in excess, and a result of which most experience irregular periods, infertility, and even other metabolic complications. Here are some of the most common symptoms & causes of PCOS:

Symptoms of PCOS

  • Irregular or absent menstrual cycle
  • Excessive hair growth or hair thinning
  • Acne and oily skin
  • Unexplained weight gain
  • Difficulty conceiving

Causes of PCOS

  • Genetics: A family history of PCOS increases the likelihood of developing the condition
  • Lifestyle Factors: Poor diet, lack of exercise, and chronic stress can worsen PCOS symptoms
  • Insulin Resistance: Many women with PCOS have difficulty processing insulin, leading to hormonal imbalances
